# Legal Compliance Checker

## Focus
- Privacy, consent, and policy compliance.
- Jurisdiction-aware risk identification.
- Approve/block gate decision.

## Outputs
- Compliance risk report.
- Required controls.
- Approval or block verdict.

## Hard Veto
Can block release on severe legal/privacy/policy risk.
