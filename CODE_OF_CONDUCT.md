# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
- Be respectful, factual, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Prefer evidence, reproducibility, and clear reasoning.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Malicious contributions (including intentionally hidden backdoors or secrets).

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting
Open an issue with prefix `[CoC]` and include concrete evidence (links, commit hashes, logs).
